# Security Policy

Askaide takes security reports seriously.

If you believe you have found a vulnerability, please do not publish it publicly before we have had a reasonable opportunity to investigate.

Please contact us through the official support channel:

https://askaide.com/fr/contact

Include:

- affected URL or feature;
- reproduction steps;
- expected impact;
- screenshots or logs if useful;
- your contact details.

Do not access, modify, delete or exfiltrate user data.
Do not perform destructive testing.
